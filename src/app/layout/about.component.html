Improve and promote the CoR's political impact

New ways of working

KIKLOS is the new online tool that enables you to easily create, edit, plan, communicate and follow up on the Committee of the Regions' political work. Its overall aim is to improve and promote the CoR's political impact.

After analysing different user profiles, investigating workflows and listening to your input, we are now building a system to facilitate your daily work.

KIKLOS is evolving

Directorate A has been working together with the Directorates of Legislative Works, Logistics and IT to bring you this new tool.

The first version was launched on 3 February 2016. Since then, KIKLOS has been constantly evolving and new features are regularly added.
